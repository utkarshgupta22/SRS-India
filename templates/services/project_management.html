MaRS has been involved with Project Management services in different fields of Infrastructure since its inception. MaRS provides integrated Project Management Services including Tasks ranging from Planning & Monitoring, Client/ Contractor/ Agency coordination, Procurement, Regular Supervision, Quality Assurance & Quality Control, Verification & Approval of Contractor’s Invoices, Assisting in Claims management, Resolving disputes, Final Bill preparation, and Final Report submission to various Government Authorities as well as Contracting firms. MaRS also provides the services for supervision of works during the Operation & Maintenance of the Assets in various Infrastructure fields. MaRS strives to deliver the projects within schedule, in compliance with the budget, and exceed the quality requirements. More recently, MaRS has started offering Building Information Modelling – based project management services in India.
